About

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Science & Engineering Department at National Institute of Technology Silchar. My specialisation lies in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and my broad research interests are in topics like Mathematical Information retrieval, Caption Generation and Text Summarization.

I aspire to create computing solutions with more insights into human behaviour using machine/deep learning techniques. I am working on my thesis in “Formula Retrieval from Scientific Documents” under the supervision of Dr. Partha Pakray and Prof. Sivaji Bandyopadhyay.
